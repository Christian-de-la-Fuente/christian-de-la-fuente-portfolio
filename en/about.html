About

My name is Christian de la Fuente, I am a 24-year-old individual, originally from Barcelona and the son of Argentinians. Since 2015, I have made my home in Turin, a city that has offered me new opportunities and enriching experiences.

From a young age, I found myself immersed in the world of technology and computing. I grew up surrounded by computers, fascinated by their complexity and potential. Although this world initially seemed foreign to me, my insatiable curiosity led me to explore it further.

Some years ago, I decided to embark on a self-taught journey in learning programming. However, as is common in life, various responsibilities and circumstances led me down different paths, and some of that knowledge faded into obscurity. Nevertheless, my interest in this field never waned.

After a period of reflection, I decided to resume my path towards programming. I started with a short course in Python and Raspberry Pi microcontrollers, which reignited my interest in this fascinating field. Then, I immersed myself in a more comprehensive course at ENGIM, where I acquired the tools and skills necessary to delve even deeper into this exciting world.

Today, I find myself here, ready to take on new challenges and opportunities in the field of programming. Although my journey has had its ups and downs, each experience has been crucial in my path towards excellence in this constantly evolving field.
